Master-Planned Neighborhoods

Prairie Trail

Ankeny's crown jewel, Prairie Trail represents the future of master-planned community development. This walkable, mixed-use neighborhood features modern amenities, trail systems, parks, and retail centers. Homes range from townhomes to custom estates. The development emphasizes sustainability, active lifestyles, and community connection. Prairie Trail attracts buyers seeking the best in contemporary suburban design.

Northwest Ankeny

Quieter, more spacious neighborhoods with larger lots and natural settings. Perfect for families seeking peace and nature access while remaining in an expanding community. This area features established residential patterns with newer development filling in. More affordable than Prairie Trail while maintaining excellent schools and access to city services.

Northeast Ankeny & Centennial

Combines newer construction homes with proximity to excellent schools and shopping. Centennial and Aspen Ridge feature modern subdivisions with quality builders and contemporary home designs. These neighborhoods appeal to young families prioritizing school access and new construction. Strong appreciation potential due to strategic location and excellent demographics.
