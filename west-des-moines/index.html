Schools & Education Excellence

Valley High School - Rating: 9/10

Reputation: One of Iowa's most prestigious public high schools, Valley consistently ranks among the state's best. The school is known for rigorous academics, excellent test scores, and comprehensive college preparatory programming.

Why Families Choose Valley: Exceptional athletic programs (state championship-caliber teams), advanced placement offerings, strong STEM initiatives, and a prestigious alumni network. The school culture emphasizes academic achievement and well-rounded development.

Notable Features: New state-of-the-art facilities, extensive arts programming, nationally recognized debate team, engineering pathways, and strong scholarship outcomes.

West Des Moines Community School District Overall

District Excellence: Consistently rated among Iowa's top-performing school districts. The district attracts experienced educators and maintains low student-to-teacher ratios. Innovation in curriculum design and early intervention programs ensures student success.

Advanced Programming: The district offers honors programs, gifted education, international baccalaureate tracks, and extensive extracurricular activities across all grade levels. Strong special education services and support for diverse learners.

Community Advantage: High median household income in the district enables strong parental engagement, well-funded programs, and facilities that rival private institutions.
