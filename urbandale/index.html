Urbandale is Iowa's quintessential suburban community, known for its exceptional parks system and outdoor lifestyle. With 68% single-family detached homes, the city offers the space families desire with the convenience of proximity to Des Moines.

Neighborhoods to Explore

Established Neighborhoods

Urbandale's original residential areas feature mature trees, well-maintained homes, and strong community connections. These neighborhoods offer the character and stability that families value, with convenient access to schools, parks, and shopping. Perfect for buyers seeking an established community with proven appreciation.

Newer Subdivisions

Contemporary developments in Urbandale showcase modern home designs with updated amenities and energy-efficient features. These neighborhoods appeal to buyers wanting newer construction with contemporary floor plans, smart home technology, and close proximity to the expanding retail district along 86th Street.

Near Living History Farms

The areas surrounding the Living History Farms combine rural character with suburban convenience. These neighborhoods offer larger lots, wooded settings, and proximity to nature while maintaining easy access to Urbandale's excellent city services and trails. Ideal for nature-loving families and retirees.

Schools & Education

Johnston Community School District

Coverage Area: Includes portions of Urbandale and surrounding areas

Why Families Choose It: Rated A+ by the state, the Johnston district offers excellent academic programming, strong extracurricular activities, and dedicated educators. The district serves over 13,000 students and maintains a reputation for academic excellence and college preparatory courses.

Notable Features: Advanced placement programs, strong athletic programs, comprehensive special education services, and modern facilities.

West Des Moines Community School District

Coverage Area: Western portions of Urbandale

Why Families Choose It: One of the state's premier districts, West Des Moines schools consistently rank among Iowa's best. The district emphasizes innovation, technology integration, and personalized learning for all students.

Notable Features: STEM programs, 1:1 technology initiatives, strong music and arts programming, and excellent athletic facilities.

What Makes Urbandale Unique

Trail Paradise

The city's 54-mile trail system is one of Iowa's most extensive networks. Connecting parks, schools, and residential areas, these trails are perfect for walking, biking, and running. The Greenbelt offers scenic views and peaceful recreation space for all seasons.

Living History Farms

This unique outdoor museum preserves Iowa agricultural heritage with demonstrations and historic sites. Open seasonally, it's an exceptional educational resource for families and a beloved community landmark that showcases the region's farming heritage.

Family-Friendly Amenities

From splash pads and playgrounds to sports facilities and community centers, Urbandale prioritizes family recreation. The city's commitment to parks and recreation creates vibrant neighborhoods where families naturally connect and children thrive.

Convenient Location

Positioned perfectly between Des Moines and suburban areas, Urbandale offers the suburban peace you want with quick access to metro amenities. I-35 and 86th Street provide convenient commuting to major employment centers throughout the metro.

Affordability

While maintaining excellent quality of life, Urbandale offers better value than some neighboring suburbs. You get outstanding schools, parks, and safety with reasonable home prices\—a rare combination in today's market.

Community Events

The city hosts regular community events including farmers markets, outdoor concerts, and holiday celebrations. These gatherings foster neighborhood connections and create a strong sense of belonging for new and longtime residents alike.
